About Us

 
 Epiphany Games is an arm under Epiphany, the purpose of which is to nurture talent in the gaming industry, showcase wonder women of the gaming world and to advocate for gaming in emerging economies
 
 

Our Initiatives
 

Celebrity Game Jam

Interactive AMAs with actors, musicians, media personalities, athletes, bankers, politicians, entrepreneurs, investors while they play pre-selected games

Wonder Women in the Gaming World
Insightful talks where women in games share their experiences & provide guidance to new entrants as well as attract more female talent towards gaming
 

 Developers' Game Jam

Time-sensitive events where everyone can create games, publish them online, learn from experts & win exciting prizes
